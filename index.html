Summary

I am an accomplished Data Scientist with a PhD in Computer Science, specializing in Machine Learning. My extensive experience spans several cutting-edge domains, including multimodal language models, generative diffusion models, trajectory reconstruction, information extraction, and language modeling. Leveraging my expertise, I have contributed to significant advancements in Visual Question Answering, Pattern Recognition, and Natural Language Processing. Throughout my career, I have held reputable research and academic positions at esteemed institutions such as the University of Rouen Normandy, INSA Rennes, CY Cergy Paris Université, and Huawei RTC Finland. My work is driven by a passion for innovative solutions and a commitment to advancing the field of machine learning and data science.
